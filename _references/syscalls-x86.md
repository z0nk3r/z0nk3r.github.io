*AKA: `IA-32`, `x86-32`.*

*CPU ISAs: `i386`, `i486`, `i586`, `i686`.*

## Calling Convention

| Syscall NR | Return | arg0 | arg1 | arg2 | arg3 | arg4 | arg5 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `eax` | `eax` | `ebx` | `ecx` | `edx` | `esi` | `edi` | `ebp` |

Invoked via `int $0x80`.
