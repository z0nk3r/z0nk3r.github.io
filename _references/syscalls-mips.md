*AKA: `MIPS`, `MIPS I`, `MIPS32`, `mipsbe`.*

*CPU ISAs: `mips`, `mipsel`.*

## Calling Convention

| Syscall NR | Return | arg0 | arg1 | arg2 | arg3 | arg4 | arg5 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `v0` | `v0` | `a0` | `a1` | `a2` | `a3` | stack | stack |
{: style="--table-col-1: 12.5%; --table-col-2: 12.5%; --table-col-3: 12.5%; --table-col-4: 12.5%; --table-col-5: 12.5%; --table-col-6: 12.5%; --table-col-7: 12.5%; --table-col-8: 12.5%;"}

Invoked via `syscall`. Only 4 args fit in registers under the o32 ABI â€” arg4 and arg5 are passed on the stack, not in registers. `a3` doubles as a boolean error flag: nonzero means `v0` holds a positive errno instead of the return value.
